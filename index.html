I have been working as a consultant with different clients for the past 2 years on data visualization projects including: Dow Jones/Wall Street Journal [1], Columbia University [2], Noom Inc. Tagasauris, Done Inc. etc.

The most resent project is the development of Lenses [1], a data transformation and visualization framework, with News Corp and NYC Media Lab. Other than those I work on different other personal and open source projects, including the development of p5.js [3] and related tools.

I enjoy it the most when I stretch it a lot to combine something I get inspired by to my own skills and interests and try to answer questions by creating. Questions like how Beckett would have written Waiting for Godot if he was a programmer [4], or how beings, represented as humans knowledge, would looks like [5], or what is a reverse-hypertext (opposite of Borges' garden of forking paths) [6]. or what is inhuman art? [7]

My background is in Computer science with focus on ML and NLP. I moved to NYC[9] 4 years ago to study Media Studies [8] at the New School.
